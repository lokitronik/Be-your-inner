  <section style="margin-bottom: 4rem;">
    <h2>A letter for you</h2>
    <p>Sometimes, the answers we seek are not outside. They are in that quiet voice that lives inside you, waiting to be heard.</p>
    <p>The pendulum is not magic or divination. It is a tool that amplifies your intuition, an extension of your own energy field that helps you connect with what you already know deep down.</p>
    <p>This guide is designed for you to start from a place of calm, respect, and openness. Take the time you need. There is no rush. The path to your interior always begins with a breath.</p>
    <div style="border-left: 2px solid #3A4D3F; padding-left: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0; font-style: italic; color: #3A4D3F;">
      The pendulum does not have the power. The power has always been in you.
    </div>
  </section>

  <section style="margin-bottom: 4rem;">
    <div style="font-family: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif; font-size: 3rem; color: #3A4D3F; opacity: 0.5; line-height: 1;">01</div>
    <h2 style="margin-top: 0;">CHOOSE YOUR PENDULUM</h2>
    <p>There is no universal pendulum. There is yours and everyone else's. The most important thing is that when you have it in your hands, something inside you says: <strong>this one</strong>.</p>
    <p>You can find them in crystal, wood, metal, or semi-precious stones. You can also improvise with something of yours, like a ring hanging from a fine thread. What matters is not the material, but the connection.</p>
    <h3>How to know which one is yours?</h3>
    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Hold it in your hand and observe how it makes you feel.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Does it transmit calm? Does it generate confidence? That is your answer.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> If you have several, you can try them one by one without thinking too much.</li>
    </ul>
    <div style="border-left: 2px solid #3A4D3F; padding-left: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0; font-style: italic; color: #3A4D3F;">
      Your energy already knows which one it is. Trust that first impression.
    </div>
  </section>

  <section style="margin-bottom: 4rem;">
    <div style="font-family: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif; font-size: 3rem; color: #3A4D3F; opacity: 0.5; line-height: 1;">02</div>
    <h2 style="margin-top: 0;">PREPARE THE SPACE AND ENERGY</h2>
    <p>Before starting, take a moment to prepare yourself. This small ritual is not mandatory, but it does make a big difference. It tells your mind that it is time to listen.</p>
    <h3>Preparation ritual:</h3>
    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Clean your pendulum with intention: you can use incense smoke or salt water (if the material allows it).</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Find a quiet place where you won't be interrupted for a few minutes.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Breathe deeply, close your eyes, and feel your body. Your feet on the ground. Your heart.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> When you feel centered, you can begin.</li>
    </ul>
    <div style="border-left: 2px solid #3A4D3F; padding-left: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0; font-style: italic; color: #3A4D3F;">
      You don't need a perfect altar or ideal conditions. Only your presence.
    </div>
  </section>

  <section style="margin-bottom: 4rem;">
    <div style="font-family: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif; font-size: 3rem; color: #3A4D3F; opacity: 0.5; line-height: 1;">03</div>
    <h2 style="margin-top: 0;">HOW TO HOLD THE PENDULUM</h2>
    <p>The way you hold the pendulum directly affects the quality of the movement. Here are some key points for it to flow clearly.</p>
    <h3>The correct position:</h3>
    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Sit comfortably with both feet on the ground.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Use the hand that feels most natural to you.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Support your elbow on a table or firm surface to reduce involuntary movements.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Hold the cord between your thumb and index finger, leaving about 8 cm in length.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Allow the rest of the cord to rest gently in the palm of your hand.</li>
    </ul>
    <h3>What to avoid:</h3>
    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">◦</span> Do not hold it from the end of the chain; the movement will be too wide.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">◦</span> Do not leave too little cord; the pendulum will not have freedom to move.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">◦</span> Do not wrap the cord around your fingers; that restricts natural movement.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section style="margin-bottom: 4rem;">
    <div style="font-family: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif; font-size: 3rem; color: #3A4D3F; opacity: 0.5; line-height: 1;">04</div>
    <h2 style="margin-top: 0;">CONNECT WITH YOUR PENDULUM</h2>
    <p>Before asking questions, you need to establish a common language with your pendulum. Each one responds differently, so this step is important.</p>
    <h3>Step-by-step calibration:</h3>
    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Calmly, say aloud or mentally: "Show me what a YES is like." Observe the movement.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Repeat with: "Show me what a NO is like." Note or memorize the direction.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Finally: "Show me when I don't know or it's not clear."</li>
    </ul>
    <p>It's normal for movements to be subtle at first. With practice, they become clearer and more fluid. Be patient with yourself.</p>
    <div style="border-left: 2px solid #3A4D3F; padding-left: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0; font-style: italic; color: #3A4D3F;">
      The language of the pendulum is subtle, like the soul. Not everything is said out loud.
    </div>
  </section>

  <section style="margin-bottom: 4rem;">
    <div style="font-family: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif; font-size: 3rem; color: #3A4D3F; opacity: 0.5; line-height: 1;">05</div>
    <h2 style="margin-top: 0;">HOW TO ASK QUESTIONS</h2>
    <p>The clarity of your questions directly influences the clarity of the answers. This is perhaps the most important step of the entire practice.</p>
    <h3>Principles for asking well:</h3>
    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Formulate closed questions whenever you can, those that are answered with yes or no.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Be specific. Avoid vague or double-meaning questions.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Do not ask if you are emotionally altered; wait until you are calm.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Ask only one question at a time and wait for the answer before continuing.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> If there is no clear answer, it may not be the time. Respect that.</li>
    </ul>
    <div style="border-left: 2px solid #3A4D3F; padding-left: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0; font-style: italic; color: #3A4D3F;">
      Do not try to control the answer. Your job is to ask; the pendulum's job is to respond.
    </div>
  </section>

  <section style="margin-bottom: 4rem;">
    <div style="font-family: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif; font-size: 3rem; color: #3A4D3F; opacity: 0.5; line-height: 1;">06</div>
    <h2 style="margin-top: 0;">IMPORTANT TIPS</h2>
    <p>The pendulum is a tool for self-knowledge, not an oracle that replaces your own criteria. Here are some reminders to maintain a healthy relationship with this practice.</p>
    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Use it as an internal guide, not as the only source of decisions.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Clean your pendulum regularly, especially if you feel it is charged or heavy.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Do not use it obsessively; if you do, take a conscious break.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Avoid asking about third parties without having had a conversation with them about it first.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> If an answer generates doubts, it is valid to revisit the question at another time.</li>
    </ul>
    <div style="border-left: 2px solid #3A4D3F; padding-left: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0; font-style: italic; color: #3A4D3F;">
      The pendulum is a mirror. It shows you what you already know but haven't dared to see yet.
    </div>
  </section>

    <h2 style="margin-top: 0;">CLOSING AND GRATITUDE</h2>

    <ul style="list-style: none; padding-left: 0;">
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Give thanks to your intuition, to your inner guide, to whatever resonates with you.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Store your pendulum carefully in a special place, it can be next to crystals or in a soft cloth.</li>
      <li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><span style="color: #3A4D3F; margin-right: 0.5rem;">✦</span> Take a moment before continuing with your day. A couple of conscious breaths are enough.</li>
    </ul>

      <span style="font-size: 2rem; color: #3A4D3F;">✦</span>
      <h3 style="margin-top: 1rem;">Remember this, always:</h3>
      <p>When you connect with yourself, everything is revealed. The pendulum does not have the power. You do. You have always had it.</p>
